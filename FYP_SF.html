Figure 4.12. Visualization of 2D interaction between (a) CMNPD30311, (b) CMNPD30314, (c) CMNPD30308, (d) CMNPD30312, (e) CMNPD30307, (f) CMNPD30310, (g) CMNPD30313, (h) CMNPD30309 and S. agalactiae phosphopentomutase by using Biovia Discovery Studio visualizer. Key amino acid residues that interact with MNPs are represented in circles. Grey colour highlights the MNPs ligand; green colour dotted line indicates conventional hydrogen bonds; medium green colour dotted line indicates van der Waals interactions; light green colour dotted line indicates carbon-hydrogen bonds and light purple colour indicates both alkyl and pi-alkyl interactions. Green colour amino acids represent conventional hydrogen bond interactions; light green colour amino acids without interactions represent van der Waals interactions and the red colour amino acids indicate unfavourable interaction with the formation of unfavourable bumps. The blue halo that surrounds the interacting residues represents the solvent-accessible surface that is proportional to its diameter.
